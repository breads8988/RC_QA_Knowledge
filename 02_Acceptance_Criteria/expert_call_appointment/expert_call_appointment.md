## User Story

**As a** signed-in Repaircheck web-app user who needs a vehicle appraisal
**I want** to find a nearby appraisal expert, filter them by rating and distance, and either request a callback appointment or schedule a video call
**So that** I can arrange an assessment of my vehicle with the right expert.

**Source:** `RC-112` — *Web App – Fix Find an Expert page* (child of Epic `RC-101` Web App). Grounded against the Figma screenshots in `01_SRS/expert_call_appointment/figma` (Home → option chooser → expert list "Experte für Fahrzeuggutachten" → filter → callback popup / booking confirmation, and the Calendly scheduling flow).

## Open Questions / Assumptions (BA — confirm with stakeholders before TC generation)

1. **✅ DECIDED (2026-07-05) — "Get appointment" and "Schedule a call" are two separate flows.** "Get appointment" (Make an appointment) submits a **callback request** (workshop contacts the user); "Schedule a video call" (Schedule a call) opens **Calendly**. They are not equivalent — RC-112's text that both open Calendly is superseded. Captured in AC-ECA-08/09/12 and BR-ECA-04.
2. **✅ DECIDED (2026-07-05) — Per-expert Calendly link.** Each expert/workshop has its **own** Calendly scheduling page (not one shared `alextyl/jaekelgmbh` link). Captured in BR-ECA-05 / AC-ECA-12.
3. **✅ DECIDED (2026-07-05) — Distance = map distance from user's current location to the workshop.** The distance on each expert card is measured on the map from the user's **current point** to the workshop's location. Captured in BR-ECA-03. **Follow-up:** confirm the fallback when the user denies location permission (no distance? prompt? default region?).
4. **✅ DECIDED (2026-07-05) — Lawyer page (RC-116) stays separate.** RC-116 ("Fix Lawyer page" — Unfall Rechtsberatung) is **not** folded into this feature; it will get its own AC spec.
5. **Empty-state UI (AC-ECA-07).** The design does not show a "no experts match" state. Confirm the expected empty-state message/behaviour when filters exclude every expert.
6. **Option chooser gating (AC-ECA-01).** Confirm whether "Next" is disabled until an option ("Schedule a video call" / "Make an appointment") is selected, and whether one is pre-selected by default.
7. **Filter semantics.** Confirmed: rating filter = *minimum* rating (≥ selected stars), range **0–5**, matched by the rating's **integer part** (1.0–1.9 → 1★, 4.0–4.9 → 4★ — not rounded); distance filter = *maximum* distance (≤ selected km), range **0–100 km**. **Follow-up:** confirm whether filters apply live or on an explicit "Apply".
8. **Callback request persistence (AC-ECA-09).** Confirm what "Yes, contact me" actually does server-side (creates a request record? notifies the workshop?) and whether the user can see/track the request afterward.
9. **Video-call duration & timezone (BR-ECA-06).** Calendly shows a fixed 1-hour slot and a timezone selector. Confirm the duration is fixed at 1 hour and how timezone is defaulted for German users.
10. **SRS note.** No `01_SRS/expert_call_appointment/epic.md` exists yet; the SRS ref points at the folder. Create the SRS note if the team wants a formal SRS anchor.
